Cropping in Photoshop

How it all works

Steps:

  1. Find, and save, high quality images of your subject (the image you wish to) and the background you wish to set it in.


  2. Next, open your images in Photoshop.


  3. Crop your subject with the "Quick Selection" tool by holding the right mouse button around the area you wish to crop (it will highlight the relative area you need).


  4. Right click the selected area (as denoted by a dotted lining) and select the "Layer via Copy" to create a new layer for the section you wish to crop.


  5. Next, drag the layer you just made to your background image (if it's too big, don't worry, we will resize it)
  6. Resize, blend, and rotate the new layer4 (as needed) to make it look as natural to the background layer you wish to place it. Then, save the image under a ".jpg" format so that you can send it to your friends.
